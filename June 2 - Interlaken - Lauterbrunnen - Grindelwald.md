
# 2 June 2026

**Route:** Männedorf → Interlaken → Lauterbrunnen → Grindelwald → Männedorf


## Overall Strategy

| Place         | Purpose                | Time      |
|---------------|------------------------|-----------|
| Interlaken    | Scenic transit stop    | ~1 hour   |
| Lauterbrunnen | Waterfalls + valley    | ~3 hours  |
| Grindelwald   | Mountain fun + views   | ~4 hours  |

---

## Swiss Travel Pass Coverage

**Fully Included:**
- ✅ Männedorf → Zürich HB
- ✅ Zürich HB → Interlaken Ost
- ✅ Interlaken Ost → Lauterbrunnen
- ✅ Lauterbrunnen → Zweilütschinen → Grindelwald
- ✅ Grindelwald → Interlaken Ost → Zürich HB → Männedorf

**Partially Covered / Need Purchase:**
- ⚠️ Grindelwald First gondola (usually ~50% discount with Swiss Travel Pass)

**Not Included:**
- ❌ First Flyer Zipline
- ❌ Trottibike Scooter
- ❌ Food/drinks

## Detailed Hour-by-Hour Plan

### 6:00 AM — Wake Up
Quick breakfast at Airbnb. Recommended:
- Coffee/tea
- Bread
- Fruits
- Sandwiches for train
_Do NOT waste time with heavy breakfast outside._

---
### 6:20 AM — Leave Airbnb
Walk to Männedorf railway station

---
### 6:33 AM — Train 1: Männedorf → Zürich HB
S7 / S20 (example)  
**Included in Swiss Travel Pass** ✅  
Travel time: ~25–30 min

---
### 7:02 AM — Train 2 (Main Train): Zürich HB → Interlaken Ost
IC81 (via Bern, Thun, Spiez)  
**Included in Swiss Travel Pass** ✅

_This is your major scenic train._

**Train Tips:**
- Sit on LEFT side after Bern for best mountain/lake scenery.

**Major Stops:**
| Stop        | What You’ll See         |
|-------------|------------------------|
| Bern        | Swiss capital          |
| Thun        | Lake + mountains       |
| Spiez       | Beautiful lake town    |
| Interlaken  | Alps region begins     |

---
### 9:00 AM — Arrive Interlaken
Exit: Interlaken Ost station

---
### 9:00–10:00 AM — Interlaken Stop
Walk to Höhematte Park

**Things to do:**
- Mountain photos
- Coffee
- Restroom break
- Children relaxation

_Do NOT overexplore here. Interlaken is your “transition stop.”_

---
### 10:05 AM — Train 3: Interlaken Ost → Lauterbrunnen
Berner Oberland-Bahn (BOB)  
**Included in Swiss Travel Pass** ✅  
Travel: ~20 minutes (very beautiful valley ride)

---
### 10:30 AM — Arrive Lauterbrunnen

---
### 10:30 AM–12:30 PM — Lauterbrunnen Exploration
**Main Activities:**
1. Staubbach Falls (must visit, easy family walk, iconic view)
2. Village walk
3. River walk
4. Photography

---
### 12:30 PM — Lunch
Recommended: pizza, pasta, burgers, fries (good family options near station). Relax properly here.

---
### 1:20 PM — Train 4: Lauterbrunnen → Zweilütschinen
BOB Regional Train  
**Included in Swiss Travel Pass** ✅

---
### 1:35 PM — Change Train at Zweilütschinen
You MUST switch trains here. Follow signs: “Grindelwald” (very easy transfer)

---
### 1:40 PM — Train 5: Zweilütschinen → Grindelwald
BOB Regional Train  
**Included in Swiss Travel Pass** ✅

---
### 2:00 PM — Arrive Grindelwald
This is the FUN part of the day.

---
### 2:00–2:20 PM — Walk to Grindelwald Firstbahn Gondola Station
Approx: 10–15 minute walk OR local bus

---
### 2:30 PM — Gondola: Grindelwald → First
**Not fully included** ❌ (Swiss Travel Pass gives discount ⚠️)
Buy tickets here. Children often get major discounts/free with Family Card.

---
### 3:00 PM — Arrive Grindelwald First

**Main Activity:**
- First Cliff Walk (included after gondola ticket ✅, safe for families, amazing mountain panorama)

**Optional Activities:**
| Activity            | Recommendation         |
|---------------------|-----------------------|
| First Flyer Zipline | YES if group energetic|
| Trottibike          | Optional              |
| Long hikes          | NO                    |
| Multiple thrill rides| NO                   |

_For your group: Choose ONLY ONE paid activity. Otherwise children become exhausted._

---
### 4:30 PM — Snack / Hot Chocolate
Relax and enjoy scenery. This is one of the best photo locations of your entire Europe trip.

---
### 5:15 PM — Return Gondola: First → Grindelwald

---
### 5:45 PM — Train 6: Grindelwald → Interlaken Ost
**Included in Swiss Travel Pass** ✅

---
### 6:30 PM — Train 7: Interlaken Ost → Zürich HB
IC81  
**Included in Swiss Travel Pass** ✅

---
### 8:30 PM — Zürich HB
Change to S7 / S20 toward Männedorf  
**Included** ✅

---
### 9:15 PM — Arrive Back Männedorf
Perfect ending. Children will sleep instantly.

---
## Final Advice

**DO THIS:**
- ✅ Interlaken short stop
- ✅ Lauterbrunnen relaxed exploration
- ✅ Grindelwald First Cliff Walk

**SKIP:**
- ❌ Jungfraujoch
- ❌ Aggressive hiking
- ❌ Too many thrill rides

_This gives the BEST balance of:_
- Scenery
- Comfort
- Excitement
- Child friendliness
- Swiss Alps experience
