## Day 2 — 3 June — Lucerne + Mt. Pilatus

**7:00 AM — Breakfast**

**8:00 AM — Train to Zurich HB**

**8:35 AM — Zurich → Lucerne**
- Travel: ~45–50 minutes
- Covered by Swiss Pass

**9:45–11:30 AM — Lucerne Old Town**
- FREE: Old Town, Riverside walk, Shopping

**Main FREE Attractions:**
- Chapel Bridge
- Lion Monument

**11:30 AM — Lake Lucerne Boat Ride**
- Covered fully by Swiss Travel Pass
- Highly recommended

**12:30–1:30 PM — Lunch**
- Lakefront area
- Approximate: CHF 25–40 adults, CHF 12–20 children

**1:45 PM — Mt. Pilatus Excursion**
- Mount Pilatus: Major Switzerland mountain experience

**Pilatus Transportation:**
- Typical route: Lucerne → Alpnachstad → Cogwheel railway to Pilatus

**Mt. Pilatus Cost:**
| Ticket Type         | Price (approx)         |
|---------------------|------------------------|
| Without Swiss Pass  | CHF 80–100/adult       |
| With Swiss Pass     | ~50% discount          |
| Children            | Reduced/free depending on age |

**Is Pilatus Worth It?**
> YES for your family. You get snow possibility, alpine scenery, mountain experience, easy accessibility — without extreme hiking.

**5:30 PM — Return to Lucerne**

**6:15 PM — Train to Zurich**

**7:30–8:00 PM — Return Männedorf**
