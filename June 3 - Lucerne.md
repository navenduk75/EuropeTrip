

# 3 June 2026 — Pilatus Golden Round Trip

**Route:** Männedorf → Lucerne → Pilatus Golden Round Trip → Männedorf

---

## Swiss Travel Pass Coverage

| Transport                     | Included?           |
|-------------------------------|---------------------|
| Männedorf → Zürich HB         | ✅ YES              |
| Zürich HB → Lucerne           | ✅ YES              |
| Lucerne local buses           | ✅ YES              |
| Lake Lucerne boat             | ✅ YES              |
| Kriens bus                    | ✅ YES              |
| Pilatus cogwheel railway      | ⚠️ 50% DISCOUNT    |
| Pilatus gondola/cable car     | ⚠️ 50% DISCOUNT    |

*Swiss Travel Pass holders travel free on the boat and bus portions; Pilatus mountain transport is discounted ~50%.*

*Children with Swiss Family Card generally travel free on Pilatus routes with parents holding Swiss Travel Pass.*

---

## Important Booking Advice

- **DO NOT prebook too early.**
- Check weather 1 day before.
- Buy Pilatus mountain tickets the previous evening or same morning. *Swiss travel experts strongly recommend waiting for weather confirmation.*

## Detailed Hour-by-Hour Plan

### 6:45 AM — Wake Up

- Relaxed breakfast at Airbnb.
- Carry: sandwiches/snacks, fruits, chocolates for children

---

### 7:45 AM — Leave Airbnb

- Walk to Männedorf railway station

---

### 8:03 AM — TRAIN 1 (S7 / S20)

- **Route:** Männedorf → Zürich HB
- **Included:** ✅
- **Travel:** ~25 minutes

---

### 8:35 AM — TRAIN 2 (IR70 / IC)

- **Route:** Zürich HB → Lucerne
- **Included:** ✅
- **Travel:** ~45–50 minutes

---

### 9:30 AM — Arrive Lucerne

- Exit station toward lakefront

---

### 9:30–10:15 AM — Lucerne Quick Walk

- Visit: Chapel Bridge
- Also: Old Town, lakeside promenade, photos
- *Keep this short because Golden Round Trip takes time.*

---

### 10:30 AM — Boat Ride (Lucerne → Alpnachstad)

- **Included in Swiss Travel Pass:** ✅
- *This is part of the Golden Round Trip.*
- **Boat ride duration:** ~1 hour+
- *Children usually LOVE this section.*

**Tip:** Sit outside/on upper deck if weather is good. Amazing lake + mountain scenery.

---

### 11:45 AM — Arrive Alpnachstad

- Walk directly to Pilatus Cogwheel Railway station (very close to boat dock)

### 12:00 PM — Cogwheel Train (Pilatus Railway)

- **Route:** Alpnachstad → Pilatus
- **Not included:** ❌ (50% discount with Swiss Travel Pass ⚠️)
- *The world’s steepest cogwheel railway — one of the highlights of Switzerland.*
- **Travel:** ~35 minutes

---

### 12:45 PM — Arrive Mount Pilatus (~2,100m+)

---

### 12:45–3:00 PM — Pilatus Exploration

**Main Activities:**

- Easy panoramic walks
- Snow photos (possible in June)
- Dragon-themed areas
- Scenic viewpoints
- Relaxation

**Important:**
- DO NOT do difficult hikes. Your group size + children = relaxed exploration is best.

**Lunch Option:**
- Eat packed food **or** scenic restaurant/café
- Picnic-style eating is completely fine in open areas

---

### 3:15 PM — Dragon Ride Cable Car (Pilatus → Fräkmüntegg → Kriens)

- **Not included:** ❌ (50% discount with Swiss Travel Pass ⚠️)
- Includes: aerial cable car, panoramic gondola
- Very scenic descent

**Optional Stop:**
- At Fräkmüntegg: children play area, short relaxation, snack
- *But do NOT overextend the day.*

---

### 4:30 PM — Arrive Kriens

---

### 4:40 PM — Bus (Kriens → Lucerne station)

- **Included in Swiss Travel Pass:** ✅
- **Travel:** ~15 minutes

---

### 5:00–6:30 PM — Relaxed Lucerne Evening

Perfect time for:
- Chocolates
- Souvenirs
- Lakeside walk
- Ice cream for children
- Relaxed dinner

**Optional Quick Stop:** Lion Monument (only if energy remains)

---

### 6:35 PM — TRAIN 3 (IR70 / IC)

- **Route:** Lucerne → Zürich HB
- **Included:** ✅

---

### 7:30 PM — TRAIN 4 (S-Bahn)

- **Route:** Zürich HB → Männedorf
- **Included:** ✅

---

### 8:15–8:30 PM — Back at Airbnb

- Perfect ending. Relaxed but unforgettable day.
